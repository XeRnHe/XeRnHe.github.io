假设你是红星中学高三学生李华。你们年级将于 6 月 18 日开展以“传承”为主题的毕业季活动。请你用英文给你们班的交换生 Jim 写一封邮件，邀请他参加，内容包括：

1. 活动内容；
2. 其他相关事项。

Dear Jim,

Greetings! I’m writing to invite you to our upcoming graduation event on June 18th with the theme “Heritage”.

Aiming to celebrate our shared memories and promote the school spirit, the event features various activities. At the “Story Booth”, graduating students will share personal stories and how the school values have shaped who we are. Another highlight is the “My Legacy” fair, where we are welcome to leave behind meaningful items, such as study notes, medals from school traditional events, or heartfelt messages to inspire future students and honor the school spirit. Following that, we will plant trees as our living legacy.
	
You can check in at the lecture hall for your graduation souvenir by 9 a.m. Bring your favorite items for the fair if you like.
	
As a valued member of our class, your presence will mean a lot to us. Let’s celebrate this significant moment together!

Yours,
Li Hua

假设你是红星中学高三学生李华。你的英国好友 Jim 听说你参加了全球青少年机器人设计大赛，想了解相关情况。请你用英文给他发邮件，内容包括：

1. 介绍你的参赛作品；
2. 分享参赛感想。

Dear Jim,

Greetings! I’m glad to know you’re interested in the Global Teenager Robot Designing Competition I’ve just joined. Here are some related details that you might find appealing.

Our entry was a robot intended for use in public venues like subway stations and busy crossroads. Powered by AI technology, the robot could display real-time arrival information of subway trains on its screen and provide pedestrians with tailored directional guidance. To meet personal needs, if such robots were applied in real-life scenarios, they would bring convenience to public transportation and enhance the city’s efficiency.

This competition made me realize the true power of technology: it makes our daily lives easier and more efficient. Seeing how a well-designed robot can save people’s time and reduce confusion in busy public spaces was truly eye-opening. I’m now more convinced that even small innovations, when applied wisely, can bring great convenience to ordinary people. 

What do you think of this experience? I’m looking forward to hearing from you.
