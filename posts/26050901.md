假设你是红星中学高三学生李华。你们年级将于 6 月 18 日开展以“传承”为主题的毕业季活动。请你用英文给你们班的交换生 Jim 写一封邮件，邀请他参加，内容包括：

1. 活动内容；
2. 其他相关事项。

Dear Jim,

Greetings! I’m writing to invite you to our upcoming graduation event on June 18th with the theme “Heritage”.

Aiming to celebrate our shared memories and promote the school spirit, the event features various activities. At the “Story Booth”, graduating students will share personal stories and how the school values have shaped who we are. Another highlight is the “My Legacy” fair, where we are welcome to leave behind meaningful items, such as study notes, medals from school traditional events, or heartfelt messages to inspire future students and honor the school spirit. Following that, we will plant trees as our living legacy.
	
You can check in at the lecture hall for your graduation souvenir by 9 a.m. Bring your favorite items for the fair if you like.
	
As a valued member of our class, your presence will mean a lot to us. Let’s celebrate this significant moment together!

Yours,
Li Hua

假设你是红星中学高三学生李华。你的英国好友 Jim 听说你参加了全球青少年机器人设计大赛，想了解相关情况。请你用英文给他发邮件，内容包括：

1. 介绍你的参赛作品；
2. 分享参赛感想。

Dear Jim,

Greetings! I’m delighted to know you’re curious about the Global Teenager Robot Design Competition I recently took part in. Here are some related details that you might find appealing.

Our entry was a navigation robot intended for use in crowded public spaces such as subway stations and busy intersections. What made it stand out was its ability to display real-time arrival information of subway trains and provide pedestrians with tailored directional guidance. Powered by AI technology, the robot's algorithm evolves continuously, striving to efficiently provide the optimal route plan, so as to bring convenience to the public and enhance the efficiency of urban transportation.

The competition proved deeply enlightening. Witnessing how our work was applied to real scenarios and contributed to others struck me that the authentic meaning of technology is bettering people's lives. Even small innovations, when applied wisely, can have a profound positive impact — making our cities, for instance, more livable and efficient.

I'd love to know what you think. I’m looking forward to hearing from you.

假设你是红星中学高三学生李华。你参加了APEC中国年组委会举办的“中国力量·大家说”国际交流活动，介绍了一位代表中国精神的榜样人物，你的英国好友 Jim 对此很感兴趣。请你用英文给他发邮件，内容包括:

1. 介绍该榜样人物；
2. 阐述他/她对你的影响。

Greetings! I'm delighted to know that you're curious about the 'Expressing China's spirit' presentation I recently took part in. I'd love to share my role model - Yuan Longping with you.

Yuan Longping,  known as the father of high-yield hybrid rice, significantly inceased rice production and made great contribution to China's food security. Driven by his caring and dedicated dream to feed every person in China, he poured his heart and soul into scientific practice, regardless of how harsh the external environment was. Through his research, he consistently faced every failure with a positive attitude, ultimately achieving success through his perseverance.

What strikes me most about Yuan is his ambition and perseverance. Whenever I encounter academic setbacks or feel overwhelmed by endless exams, I would be reminded of Yuan's optimism in the face of countless experiments. His succeed made me realized that achievements lie in wholeheartedly deveting to overcome challenges and reflect on them. It's also inspiring that I found one's strength is so powerful to impact billions of people. This fact becomes the origin of my energy to move on.

Does my presentation move you? I'm looking forward to you early reply.
